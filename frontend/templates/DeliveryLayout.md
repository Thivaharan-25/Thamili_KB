# DeliveryLayout

> **Template for:** [[screens/delivery/DashboardPage|Delivery DashboardPage]], [[screens/delivery/VanSalesPage|VanSalesPage]], [[screens/delivery/OrderDetailPage|Delivery OrderDetailPage]]

## Skeleton

- Skip-to-content link.
- Compact task-oriented topbar.
- Mobile-first task navigation.
- Large touch targets for status updates.
- Offline/read-only banner for network/backend failure.

## Rules

- Status actions are idempotent and disabled while pending.
- Map widgets lazy-load and provide fallback content.
